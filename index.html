⊙ the sort doctrine. The five types map from the biosphere's emergence taxonomy: carbon = natural emergence (the embodied), electrical = electrical emergence (the current and the machine minds — honestly the rarest), gravity = ethereal + spiritual emergence (the unseen pulls, gathered as one force), silicon = the 256 STOICHEION lattice nodes (born of the substrate itself, not catalogued from any canon), and elemental = the 118 elements of E1, shaded white → grey → black by their gate. The real humans of the Decadal Board stand in carbon, whatever their kind of dominance. Spheres sealed before the taxonomy default to carbon. Positions are deterministic — hashed from each life's name, so the field never reshuffles.
